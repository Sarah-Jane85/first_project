# GREAT BARRIER REEF HEALTH

## Description
With our project we would like to draw attention to the destruction of the great barrier reef, warn about the dangers and call for action.

# Challenge 
Development of an Interactive Data Visualization Dashboard to Enhance Awareness and Encourage Individual Action for the Conservation of the Great Barrier Reef


# Hypothesis 1 
Reefs in the Great Barrier Reef experiencing higher sea surface temperatures and marine heatwaves will show greater coral bleaching severity and lower numbers of marine species observed.

# Hypothesis 2 
Human-induced climate change is the primary threat to the Great Barrier Reef

# Hypothesis 3
The destruction of the great barrier reef will exacerbate climate-related impacts.
